Frequently Asked Questions

Our portal supports filings for patents (utility and design), copyrights, trademarks, and industrial designs. We also provide guidance on trade secrets protection, though those don't require formal registration.

Our internal review typically takes 2-3 weeks. After submission to the patent office, publication usually occurs within 18 months. The entire process from filing to grant averages 3-5 years depending on the technology domain and jurisdiction.

Costs vary by IP type and jurisdiction. For Indian patents, our institutional support covers 80% of filing fees. Typical costs are:

  • Patent filing: ₹2,500-5,000 (after subsidy)
  • Copyright registration: ₹1,000-2,000
  • Trademark registration: ₹2,000-4,000

Yes, we facilitate PCT (Patent Cooperation Treaty) applications for international protection. After initial filing in India, we can help you navigate the international filing process through our global partner network.

All applications receive real-time status updates in your dashboard. You'll also receive email notifications at key milestones. For detailed examination reports, our team will contact you with guidance on responding to office actions.
